Kadapa district

Kadapa district (officially: YSR district;[4] formerly: Cuddapah district[5]) is one of the twenty six districts in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. On 19 August 2005, the nomenclature of “Cuddapah” had been changed to “Kadapa” by the Government of A.P. It was renamed as Y.S.R District during the year 2010, in honour of Y. S. Rajasekhara Reddy, the former chief minister of united Andhra Pradesh.[6] In 2022 reorganisation of Andhra Pradesh districts, the district boundary is largely restricted to Kadapa parliamentary constituency area. It is also one of the eight districts in the Rayalaseema region. Kadapa is the administrative headquarters for this district.
